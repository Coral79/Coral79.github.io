Chuqiao Li

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Tübingen & Tübingen AI Center, supervised by Prof. Dr. Gerard Pons-Moll, Prof. Dr. Andreas Geiger. My research focuses on 3D human motion synthesis and understanding, human-scene interaction, and generative AI.

Prior to this, I achieved my master degree at ETH Zurich, where I developed a strong interest in computer vision, machine learning, digital human, and scene understanding.

During my master's studies, I completed my thesis at the Visual Computing and Artificial Intelligence at MPI-INF supervised by Dr. Marc Habermann and Prof. Christian Theobalt. During my study at ETH Zurich, I also have collaboration with Prof. Luc Van Gool. I obtained my BSc from Harbin Institute of Technology.
